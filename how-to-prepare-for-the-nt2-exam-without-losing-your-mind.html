What Exactly is the NT2 Exam?

The NT2 Exam is a standardized Dutch proficiency exam, primarily required for:

  • Permanent residency applications

  • Dutch citizenship

  • Employment or higher education

  • Proof of Dutch language skills at intermediate (B1-B2) level

There are two main NT2 exams:

  • NT2 Programma I (B1 level): Intermediate proficiency for employment and vocational education.

  • NT2 Programma II (B2 level): Advanced proficiency needed for university studies and professional careers.

Both exams test practical language use, not just grammar theory. Let's break down each exam section clearly.
