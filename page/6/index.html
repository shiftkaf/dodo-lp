Why the name “Dodo”?

I struggled to learn Dutch for nearly a decade. Apps, books, classes, you name it. And every time I tried to practice, people would kindly switch to English. My Dutch wasn’t just stuck… it was going extinct.
The dodo bird famously went extinct after Dutch sailors landed on its island. So yeah. There’s a bit of poetic irony. But now, Dodo is coming back to help your Dutch thrive.
